Vanguard v.1.2

The Vanguard uses both supportive and defensive abilities to become a guarding shield to those who need it.

Features
Protective Act

As a reaction whenever a creature makes a ranged attack against an allied creature you may move up to 1 square in order to stand in the line of fire (standing between the attacked and the attacker) if you do you become the target of that attack instead.

Reactive Stance

At any time: You may convert a movement action into a reaction. (this can be done to play an additional Reaction Card during the round as long as you still have your movement action)
