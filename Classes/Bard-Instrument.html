Instrumental Bard

A bard can bind magic spells to the tune of their instrument and control the way its sound will travel wether it be to aid an ally or cause a horde of goblins to fall asleep. As a flexible magic wielder the bard can control the outcome of a battlefield.

Features
Controlled Tunes

You gain the Card 'Controlled Tunes'.

Lingering melody

Start any battle with the condition 'Captivating Song'.
Whenever this condition is removed place it sun face up attached to you instead.
