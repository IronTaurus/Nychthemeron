Phantom Lord

Features
Phantom Servant

You may take control of the soul of a fallen humanoid as a phantom. The phantom will retain all basic cards + 1AC of its class cards. They keep half their stats and their weapon is considered a normal version of the weapon they would use when alive. Their health is increased by 50%, rounded up. If their health becomes 0 you remove the phantom. Instead of using SP the phantom uses its health to cast spells. You may only keep 1 phantom at the time.
