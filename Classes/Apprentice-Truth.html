Truth Apprentice v.1.2

The Truth apprentice is a supportive class with powerful spells. It uses 'The All Seeing Eye' to get an edge against powerful enemies and grants powerful buffs to their allies.

Class Specific Skills
The all seeing eye

Gain the card: 'The All Seeing Eye' and attach it to a target creature within sight. You may spend a Standard action to attach 'the all seeing eye' to a different creature. Any moment the target creature dies: You may immedietly reattach 'The All Seeing Eye' to another creature.

Touch of a Truthsayer

Gain the card: 'Rooom of Truth / Revisited'.
