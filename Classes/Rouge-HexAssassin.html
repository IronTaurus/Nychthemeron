Hex Assassin

The Hex assassin may not be as stealthy as other assassins but they do make up for it with their agile movements and their excellent combination of corruptive curses and piercing attacks. Being able to easily weaken their enemy while swiftly moving around in the battlefield attacking their enemy from any direction.

Features
Quick and Quiet

You may use potions and salves as a free action (Once/Turn). You may ignore the verbal component for reaction spells.

Backstab

If you attack a creature without it being aware of you, you may add 2 AC in additional damage.
