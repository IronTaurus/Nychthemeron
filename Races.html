Races of Athernis

Light Elestine

Background:

The Elestine are a race that are what people call angels, they are a bewinged race living among the gods but not necessarily as their subjects. They have a strong mind and are in full control of their own thoughts. Among the Elestine there are two types, the Light Elestine who live in the bright parts of Athernis and the Dark Elestine who live in the dark parts. While they are the same their differences make them seem like two different races.

Appearence:

If the Elestine is a light Elestine their skin are usually very pale and feels almost powdery like, their eyes have almost nonexistent pupils while still having an iris. The iris can be different colors from almost all spectrums but more commonly they are silver or blue. They often have long sharp nails and large wings that enable them to fly.

Behaviour:

The Elestine have sensitive skin and they normally do not like to be touched by others. The feathers in their wings are filled with Arcane energies which allows them to fly. While they do maneuver using their wings they can normally be seen hovering only by stretching them out and do not like landing as their bare feet would then touch the hard surface of the ground.

High Elves

Background:

High Elves are a branch from the Fey Elves that built settlements near humans and learned to coexist with them. Building cities they started to adapt to their surroundings and became what they are today.

Appearence:

High Elves are known for their sharp ears, much shorter than the others of their kin but still long compared to humans. They often have golden blonde or silver white hair, there are cases where a high elf has other colors but the most known high elf would have blonde hair and long hair that they are very proud of. The skin colors are often pale, and since they always take care of their beauty they often have long nails and a face untouched by battle. High elf dress often in light clothes such as silk in bright colors, they love anything that glimmers so crystals, gold and silver are often worn as jewelry. Heavy metals on the other hand, such as iron gives the high elves a chilling sensation that makes them feel uneasy and weak.

Behaviour:

Not at all as secluded as their relatives, high elves cherish their beauty and are not shy to show it for anyone who wishes to adore it. The high elves, unlike the others of their kin, does not shy away from the other races; instead you can often find them mingling with other races even though they often consider them a race of lesser status and beauty. They have a strong affection to beauty and light, often working in high positions and/or crafting of gold, jewelry and silk. Because of their long life span they are known for being expert craftsmen.

A typical house of a High elf is that of a bright wooden house with plenty of carved or painted decorations, the house would have many large windows to let as much sunlight in as possible with silken drapes to cover them if needed. A typical high elf does not enjoy living in cold environments and in harsh sleeping conditions.

Elves perceive time differently than other races, this is mainly due to their long life span. Similar to how some people won’t notice or rather forget when they do some everyday chores an elf will feel the same about the years that pass. This can result in elves being very selective about what they remember and it is not uncommon for them to not remember names of people they don’t find worthy of remembering. Through their meditation they can handle their memories and can remember things they have forgotten with this method.

Wood Elves

Background:

Wood elves are a branch of Fey elves that have settled in the forests of Mihridahl, they have adapted to the forest and have created a strong bond with it. Amongst these elves are a tradition to travel to visit other tribes as a way to exchange history and culture.

Appearence:

Probably the closest to their origin as fey elves out of all the elves, very adaptable they can shift colors of their skin or hair according to the nature around them. This often takes years or months of time but happens gradually so some changes may be seen after only a few days. Their hair is often in the colors of leaves and grass ranging from green to orange. And when growing old they tend to get almost bronze like hair and much more gray or brown in color. They have long pointy ears.

Behaviour:

A wood elf feels the most comfortable when near nature. Their deep connection to the forest makes them almost a part of it and can often feel if anything is wrong with it. To get this bound they must live in the forest for a long time. This also causes them to be a bit overprotective towards the forest. They are often wary of other races and dislike any trespass in their forest. Despite this, Wood Elves are very reasonable and help people in need regardless of their race as long as their forest is protected. Wood elves live as one with nature. Not only do their appearance change according to the nature around them they can also prolong their lifespan by binding their life to a single tree in which they live as long as that tree.

Darklings

Background:

A Darkling or Half demon are the descendants of the unlikely spawn of a demon and another race. Although many can look much like a normal person they often have some twisted nature from their demon blood and there are several places such as Heln that reject and sometimes even hunt down those that are found out to be Darklings. There are many Darklings however and some regions have learned to live with them and try to accept them as any other race even if they get frowned upon from time to time.

Appearence:

A Darkling could have features similar to that of a typical demon and any other race. Because demons use husk created from fallen souls as their bodies, a Darkling can have many different features from other races. For example: an elf with a demon heritage could have the ears and skin/hair colors of an elf, the horns of a demon and the feathers and wings from a Elestine from whom the demon husk had its form. Thus the demon's only recognisable feature would be their twisted features and the horns they can often grow from anywhere on the body.

Behaviour:

A Darkling has the vision of a normal human but they have very keen senses that are heightened when their sight is removed such as during night however during a bright day the Darklings can often feel drowsy. They can scent other creatures in their surroundings and can even tell the difference between races by scent alone. A darkling with a strong bloodline towards their heritage feels a slight hunger that seems to never go away. Normally the Darkling with this trait learns to live with this hunger and suppress it, others try to still it by any means necessary.

Mountain Dwarves

(Fjelka Dvergner)

Background:

Mountain dwarves or Fjelka Dvergner are one of the branches of dwarves that exists in Mihridahl, the Mountain dwarves are the more common dwarf you think of when speaking about dwarves. They reside in caves near mountains and build large cities inside them. They don’t socialize with others as much as Hill dwarves but they don’t shy away as much as Cave dwarves. They are known for their expert iron smithing.

Appearence:

Mountain dwarves are generally very short but muscular. They have generally slow growth rate on their hair and beard so they tend to keep it as long as they can, you can often tell how old a dwarf is based on the length of his/her hair or beard. The hair is a lot thicker and tougher than a humans and also does not easily burn so even if accidents happen near the fire their hair is not always the first thing to burn. Mountain dwarves have various skin colors ranging from pale brown to darker warmer colors. Their hair can be seen in various natural colors often brown or blonde but also almost as dark as black.

Behaviour:

Mountain Dwarves often live in large mountains in which they hollowed out to build large dwarven cities. They are mainly miners and thus have a very keen eye to different ores they can see. They generally dislike having elves meddling with their creations, sometimes due to the conflict many years ago but mainly because of their disagreement of who is the better craftsman. They often include iron in their crafts such as decorative rings of iron in the handle to ward away elves from touching it.

Forest Orcs

(Morkka Orkkas)

Background:

Although the name is forest orcs it is a general term to describe the many orc tribes living in the forests and hills. They are a violent race in Mihridahl but this does not mean they can't negotiate with other races however this is more likely only if they can’t resolve it by force. Throughout history forest orcs have been in war with at least one race at any point in time which has caused them to be called a war loving race. There are numerous tribes in Mihridahl and while most have built villages and fortresses other tribes live like nomads pillaging places as they wander the lands. A typical forest orc shaman uses drums and ceremonial dances to cast their magic.

Appearence:

A forest orc is well known for their muscular large body towering up to 3 meters tall with tusks. A forest orc can have different skin and hair color but because they are so territorial a single tribe often only consists of a single color of skin. This is mainly because they tend to kill off any offspring with different colored skin. The reason for it can be traced back to ancient time when an orcs survival was depended on its ability to hide and blend in with the environment, in order to have a higher survival rate of the tribe they would favor the skin color that would work as camouflage in the respective environment the tribe live in and kill their offspring born with a odd colored skin.

Behaviour:

A forest orc tribe usually don’t become large and this is mainly because of their habit of fighting each other mostly for the role as a chieftain. can happen if the previous chieftain is dead or if someone feels strong enough to take the current chieftain down.

Dalin

Background:

A strange type of people much feared by other races maybe because of the fact that they can look identical to them and they kill newborns to replace them with their own, the child is then fed the blood of the newborn to take its appearance. The Dalins are a fairly young race compared to many others. Their creator ”the god of shadows”' made them after his own image walking in the form of others. A Dalin female will die within a year after having conceived twins. The shadow god would visit them to claim their life and bring their spirit through the shadows back to his domain. The shadow god collects his childrens souls and their memories rather than having them return to the world tree.

Dalins can ‘assimilate’ blood from other creatures to take their shape. This ability is not always perfect and often young Dalins would only be able to take some aspects of the creature and not the whole form. For example shaping their hands into paws, arms into wings, etc. Dalins can also seem like they have thoughts elsewhere and not always focused. This is often due to their ‘calling’ as when they are near forests they can feel the presence of something familiar and feel something calling to them A Dalin child will grow up with strange whispers in their dreams, calling to them. Later they feel the urge to find something, like if they are pulled to venture towards a place they have heard about in their dreams. What happens when they find this place is not spoken of but it is something each Dalin searches for and will never feel complete unless they have found it.

Appearence:

A Dalin are born with grey almost transparent skin with pitch black eyes. They have no gender but would instead become the gender of the child they mimic. As an adult when a Dalin has full grasp of their powers they can willingly change their gender but it is a difficult process. A Dalin can otherwise have almost any appearance but often take the shape of the race and person they first mimicked.

Behaviour:

Dalins are created to become strong and thus they in all biological aspects “fall in love” in a person that would be best for the survival of their children. What triggers this is up to the GM but a Dalin can never fall in love with a person who has a genetic disease or any other inherited weakness instead would feel a natural repulsion for such people.

Average Height: 130cm

Average Age: 220 years

Shapeshifter

Dalins have the ability to change their shape at will. You gain Shift points that can be used to transform parts of your body to a certain form based on what you have assimilated.

Genetic Attraction

Dalins, in all biological aspects, can only “fall in love” in a person that would be beneficial for their heritage. A Dalin can never fall in love with a person who has a genetic disease or any other type of inherited weaknesses, instead they would feel a natural repulsion for such people.
