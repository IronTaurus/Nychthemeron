Minarith

Minarith is in its physical form a source of energy giving light and life to the realm of Athernis, in its Astral form they are portrayed as a being with three arms one much longer than the other two with four fingers, they have one normal sized right eye and four smaller left eyes aligned vertically down their cheek. Minarith has a feminim appearence and thus is often refered to as a she, anyone claimed to have seen Minarith have describe them as a half beutiful radiating light and half grotesque instilling fear to those how lay their eyes on them.
