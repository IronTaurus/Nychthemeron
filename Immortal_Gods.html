Gods of Mihridahl

Sahrina

Old God

Domain:

Madness, Love

Sigil:

A womanly figure covering her eyes with her hands.

Description:

Sahrina was also once the Godess of light, beauty and love, she was however entranced by the life of mortals and lost herself in the process, rising up as the new goddess Sahrina who still harbours love and beauty but also madness. Her affection for the mortals became an obsession and a craving. She craves the blood of her apprentices and thus if she would see them she would lash out and eat them.

Appearence:

Sahrina is often depicted as a young beautiful woman with a blood red dress, she has long brown hair and hides her eyes or stay hidden behind a tree or on the other side of a wall.

Verisa

Old God

Domain:

Justice, Truth, Judgement

Sigil:

Black ring with two wings of opposite direction on each side and an eye that is both closed and open at the same time.

Description:

Verisa is the old god of truth and justice, was worshiped by large cities in the past and statues of her could be found inside courtrooms. Nobles however did not like her disregard of rank and with time the major cities soon replaced her statues with other gods that could favor the rich and often denied her clerics and apprentices into the courtroom. This led to Verisa becoming more favored and worshiped amongst smaller villages and the poor rather than large cities and nobles.

Appearence:

Verisa visits her worshipers when they are alone in a room, the room would become dark and start to slowly become covered by a black mist, from which a womanly figure is formed with a black gown and a beautiful face white as marble, her eyes closed shut. You would not be able to see her full body as her gown blends into the mist from her waist down. Her hands and arms are as pale as her face with long sharp nails. She speaks gently almost like a whisper and ít is said that if you lie to her, the mist becomes thicker and a black liquid would start to rise from the floor and the more you lie the deeper it becomes.

Verisa is a kindhearted and just goddess, however, she can deal a grave punishment to those who deserve it. To the small lies and crimes she can be forgiving and give out a simple warning. To the bigger or repeated crimes she is less forgiving and can steal your ability to speak in a horrid nightmare of which she digs out your tongue with her sharp nails. If the committed crime is too severe and death is your punishment, it is said to be exacted inside a nightmare where you would either drown in the black liquid representing your lies as she herself will hold you down or she will keep you in place as she uses her sharp teeth to devour you piece by piece in a demonic fashion.

Apprentice:

Often dressed in contrasting colors of dark and light, the apprentices wear a pendant of black Onyx shaped as a Ring with two wings on opposite sides and an eye that is both closed and open at the same time. A ring of words is inscribed with a simple line stating “Verisa is the light that illuminates the truth through the dark mist of lies!”. The text is often inscribed in Ancient.

To continue to spread her wisdom, a wandering order called “Truth Seekers” was formed to enact justice to those who deserve it regardless of rank. Typically they would wander the world like nomads and occasionally attend trials like a jury or just in order to make sure each member speaks truthfully. A village can call for a Truth Seeker to be present during a trial often in exchange for food, shelter during their stay and a small gift for their visit. To do this they send a request in the form of a letter leaving it inside a nearby shrine and a wandering Truth Seeker will find it in time. Not every call for a Truth Seeker will be answered.

Races of Naerini

Jorlna

Old God

Domain:

Protection, fertility

Sigil:

The insignia of Jorlna is a Meirin root on a shield, a Meirin root originates in Naerini and is very difficult to grow in Mihridahl, it has flowers with a sky blue color and is similar to a freesia in appearance, it has large roots and populate through the roots rather than seeds. An apprentice of Jorlna can often dedicate a lot of their time growing Meirin flowers in their garden so if you would see this in Mihridahl it is most likely the work of a dedicated apprentice or worshipper.

Description:

Jorlna is a goddess that is in the majority worshipped by halfkin and is even considered to be the mother of the Halflings, often described as warm hearted, kind and curious.

Appearence:

Jorlna is often described as a halfling woman wearing a green robe with blue broderie, long braided walnut colored hair and silver grey eyes.

Valeire

Old God

Domain:

Tricks, Joy

Sigil:

The insignia of Valeire is three different flowers bound by an orange ribbon. The type of flower is not important, only that they are three of different kinds and bound with an orange ribbon.

Description:

Valeire is another halfkin goddess, a very childish goddess that enjoys the small moments of life and wishes nothing else than to bring a smile to those she meets. She is playful, childish and mischievous but also goodhearted.

Appearence:

Valeire is often depicted as a childlike character, bushy wild red hair and freckles on her face. Always wearing a huge smile and when visiting her apprentices she often hides in bushes or fields and giggles until her Apprentice finds her.
